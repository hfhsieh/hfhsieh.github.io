Hi. I am He-Feng Hsieh（謝和峯）

I am a postdoc at the Institute of Astronomy of the National Tsing Hua University in Hsinchu, Taiwan, working in the group of Kuo-Chuan Pan since 2019. I am also an visiting postdoc at the Academia Sinica Institute of Astronomy and Astrophysics in Taipei, Taiwan, collaborating with Min-Kai Lin.

My work focuses on the planet-disc interaction, planet formation, and the possible gravitational wave emissions and in core-collapse supernovae.
